Bathroom & Kitchen Renovations

Full or partial remodels that transform your most-used rooms into stunning spaces.

Schedule this service

Overview

HandyTeddy handles complete bathroom and kitchen renovations, from design to completion. We manage all aspects: demolition, plumbing, electrical, tile, cabinetry, countertops, and fixtures. Our team coordinates with licensed specialists as needed, ensuring a smooth, stress-free experience. Whether you want a simple update or a full gut renovation, we’ll deliver a beautiful, functional space on time and within budget.

What's included

  • Single point of contact – we manage the entire project
  • Custom designs that reflect your style
  • High-quality materials and finishes

How it works

1

Design & Planning
