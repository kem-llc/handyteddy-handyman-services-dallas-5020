Our story

Your Trusted Handyman in Dallas

HandyTeddy’s mission is simple: provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with reliable, high-quality handyman services that make life easier. We believe in honest work, fair pricing, and lasting relationships.

HandyTeddy was founded in 2012 by Teddy Martinez, a Dallas native with a passion for fixing things and helping neighbors. What started as a one-man operation fixing leaky faucets and patching drywall quickly grew into a trusted team of skilled craftsmen. Teddy’s philosophy – treat every home like your own – became the cornerstone of the company’s reputation.

Today, HandyTeddy serves the entire Dallas metro area, from Lakewood to Uptown. Our team has grown, but our values haven’t changed. We still show up on time, communicate clearly, and take pride in every job. Whether it’s a small repair or a major renovation, we treat your home with the same care we’d give our own.
